our We Charge rates

We Charge connects you to public charging stations every month starting from just 0.- RON and offers exclusive advantageous prices for Volkswagen ID holders. With We Charge Free, We Charge Go and We Charge Plus, you can choose from three charging rates that are tailored to your individual needs - whether you're an occasional user or a frequent traveler.
